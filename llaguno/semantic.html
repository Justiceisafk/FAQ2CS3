Introduction

UD or Ultimate Disk is a team sport that involves throwing a disc and catching within a specifed area.

Players use different throwing techniques to pass the disc to their teammates

Details

I started this hobby when I was Grade 8, I really like this sport because it shows off my strengths in game. Catching disks with my speed and agility just brings me joy.
